# Wall 
**Walls** are a type of building. They provide protection against zombies and players, as nothing can pass through them. It is important to place walls around your base for an extra layer of protection, or to influencec the way the zombies move (if you have an open entrance type of base).

Compared to its counterpart - Doors - Walls have more health, but do not start to regenerate as quickly as tier 8 Doors.

## Overview
- Description: `"Blocks enemies from reaching your towers."`
- They have the size of 1x1 in-game cells.
- Requires 2 Wood to place.
- You can place 250 walls (per party member, 1000 total).
